\abstract{The paper investigates suitable recording techniques for capturing and producing a live musical performance for a virtual reality environment. For this an Ambisonic workflow was established and assessed through the use of listening tests. The listening test indicate the necessity of using a mix of microphone arrays and spot microphones to ensure better spatial audio quality leading to a more enjoyable experience.}
